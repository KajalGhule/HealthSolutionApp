###Application Details In Short
The HealthSolutionApp is a comprehensive health management application specifically designed to 
cater to the needs of individuals aged 65 and above. 
It serves as a user-friendly platform that enables senior citizens to monitor and maintain their overall well-being with ease and convenience.
the HealthSolutionApp utilizing Spring Boot as the backend, React as the frontend, and an SQL database 
aims to improve the health and well-being of seniors by providing a user-friendly interface
